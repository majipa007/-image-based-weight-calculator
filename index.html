How It Works

1. Image Segmentation: YOLO-based model identifies and segments the goat from the background.
2. Depth Estimation: MiDaS model generates a depth map to understand the 3D structure of the goat.
3. Volume Calculation: Using the segmentation mask and depth information, we calculate a volume proxy.
4. Weight Estimation: A calibrated scaling factor converts the volume proxy to estimated weight in kilograms.
